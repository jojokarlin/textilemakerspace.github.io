The Textile Makerspace located in building 260 (Pigott Hall) in room 238. It's open weekdays, with afternoon/evening and some morning hours, for anyone to come use our sewing machines, serger, embroidery machine, knitting machines, looms, and other making supplies. Curious but new to textile making? Our staff can show you how!

We're open finals week 12-6, Monday-Thursday. Summer hours coming soon!
