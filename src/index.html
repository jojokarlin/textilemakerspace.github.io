We have a new embroidery machine (Brother PE900)!

The Textile Makerspace is open weekdays, with afternoon/evening and some morning hours, for anyone to come use our sewing machines, serger, embroidery machine, knitting machines, looms, and other making supplies. Curious but new to textile making? Our staff can show you how!

Upcoming workshops

  • Wednesday, 3/13 2-4 PM (rescheduled again, this time for real): Pocket hacking workshop (Quinn Dombrowski). Got a dress with an unacceptable lack of pockets? Got pants with pockets so tiny to be useless? Bring it to the Textile Makerspace and learn how to upgrade your clothes!

Interested in the intersection of data and textiles? The Textile Makerspace will be hosting DLCL 203: Data Visualization with Textiles in spring 2024.

The Textile Makerspace is part of the Making@Stanford makerspace network.
